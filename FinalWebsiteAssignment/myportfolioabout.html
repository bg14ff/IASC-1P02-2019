This Portfolio displays some of my work over the term in IASC 1P02 - Web Media Production, and was created entirely using HTML, CSS, and Javascript code that I have learned in Lecture, Lab, W3Validator and through independent exploration of the W3School website and tutorials. My professor and TAs have been an invaluable resource both during instruction times, office hours and via email/telephone support.

Speaking as a first time coder, I truely loved this experience and I highly recommend this course to anyone interested in an introduction to web design. If this old lady can learn these new laguages, anyone can! :) This website is my final project for the course, I hope it inspires you to follow your dreams, whatever they may be!

Some of the things I have learned how to create:

  • Relative and Absolute links
  • Two Types of lists, ordered and unordered
  • Two Types of images, Relative and Absolute)
  • An in-page anchor
  • Different sized headers
  • Paragraphs with and without tag styled text, i.e., italic, bold, etc.
